Privacy Policy

How McBride Construction Ltd collects, uses and protects your information.

Last updated: January 2026. This is placeholder content for demonstration purposes and should be reviewed by McBride Construction before going live.

1. Information we collect

When you contact us through this website or by phone, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, suburb and the details of the project you've described. We only collect what we need to respond to your enquiry and quote your work.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and arrange a site visit or quote;
  • Carry out and manage any work you engage us for;
  • Keep in touch about your project; and
  • Meet our legal and record-keeping obligations.

3. Sharing your information

We do not sell your information. We may share it with trusted subcontractors or suppliers where it's needed to carry out your work, and with our advisers or authorities where required by law.

4. Storage and security

Your information is stored securely and kept only for as long as it's needed. Enquiries made through this website are delivered to us by email via a third-party form service.

5. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request corrections. Contact us to make a request.

6. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies, only where needed for basic functionality. We do not use them to identify you personally.

7. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Contact us at karl@mcbrideconstruction.co.nz or 03 377 2518.
